Welcome!

This app is to make your own app/game and publish it to the mpax235 Store.

Please read this before publishing:
1. You may only create 2 apps/games in a Day.
2. No spam apps/games.
3. The app/game must be appropriate.
4. The app/game must follow GitHub's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
5. No apps/games linking to malware.
6. If you choose to go to make your own icon, it needs to be approved or it will be going with the pre-set option.

If you break these Apps publishing guidelines, your app has a high chance of not being approved.
